Flight Planning Reimagined for Modern Operators

Aviate is a lightweight, modular flight operations platform purpose-built for ACMI, regional, and startup airlines who need power without the complexity.

Why Aviate?

  • Fast, intuitive UI – no training manual required
  • Built for real-world ops – irregular schedules, ACMI leases, short turnarounds
  • Modular – use only what you need: planning, dispatch, scheduling, or crew tools
  • Interoperable – export to PDF, CSV, or integrate via REST API

Core Modules

  • Flight Planning Assistant: Route planning, ETP/ETOPS, fuel breakdowns
  • Fleet Scheduling Timeline: Gantt-style planning with aircraft and crew assignments
  • Aircraft Profiles: Reusable performance templates for faster dispatch
  • Dispatch Brief Generator: One-click PDF for pilots and crew

Built For

  • Charter & ACMI operators
  • Startup low-cost carriers
  • Regional airlines
  • Dispatch training schools
